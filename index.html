Our Values

Collaboration /kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn/ n. the act of working with another person or group of people to create or produce something; a confluence of building and creating something that can not be achieved individually.

Craftsmanship /ˈkrɑːftsmənʃɪp/ n. the level of skill shown by somebody in making something beautiful with their hands;beauty of handmade work that can transcend the physical world into the digital.

Creatio ex nihilo /eks-ˈnē-(h)ə-ˌlō /adj., adv. Latin for "creation out of nothing"; even a digital world finds its roots in the physical world. Re-valuing our physical world to enhance our digital lives is at the heart of Piksel.
