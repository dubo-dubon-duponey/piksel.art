Our Mission / Our Values


Piksel is building the bridge between creators and the digital world.


Collaboration
We don't build things alone. We thrive on the confluence of artists, creators, builders, inventors, dreamers. Our approach is human to human - putting together their collective efforts to create something they could not have done alone. Piksel’s ambition is to connect the dots between people and cuttingedge technology.

Craftsmanship
Craftsmanship has long been the sole property of the physcial world. Nowadays, the emergence of a digital craftsmanship is consuming the world in the name of digital collectible art. We continue the tradition by creating a bridge between the classical world the the artist’s landscape of tomorrow.

Creatio Ex Nihilo
There is no such a thing as a ex-nihilo digital world. The digital world finds its roots in our physical world. To re-value our physical world to enhance our digital life is at the heart of Piksel. Artists and creators of any kind can offer experiences beyond just their physical creations. Piksel provides the digital key to unlock the limitless possibilities NFTs can bestow.
